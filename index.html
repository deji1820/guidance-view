The HIMC Disciplinary
Measures

Counseling Referral

LEVEL 1 - Negligence to Fulfill Basic Duties

  • Tardiness
  • Unexpected absences
  • Wearing inappropriate attire
  • Failure to submit correspondence / have materials a 3rd time
  • Sleeping in class
  • Violation of SOP's
  • Misbehavior
  • Improper haircut
  • No ID or No gate pass
  • Loitering during class hours
  • Littering
  • Trespassing
  • Disruption of classes.

Penalties for Level 1 Offenses

  • 1st Offense - Reminder + 1 demerit
  • 2nd Offense - Reminder + 2 demerits
  • 3rd Offense - Reprimand + 3 demerits
  • 4th Offense onwards - Parent Notification + 4 demerits

LEVEL 2 - Minor Behavioral Infractions or Violations of Basic School Rules and Regulations

  • Any disruptive behavior/audio/video/material or physical appearance which distracts/affects co-students or the whole class
  • Using vulgar language
  • Refusal to do assigned tasks/participate in class discussions and/or join in school-related activities
  • Bringing electronic gadgets inside the classroom
  • Vandalism
  • Putting temporary marks on their bodies
  • Misdemeanor (i.e. PDA, talking back to authorities and elders, showing disrespectful gestures, etc.)
  • Loitering outside premises during class hours or vacant time
  • Use of gadgets without permission

Penalties for Level 2 Offenses

  • 1st Offense - Parent Notification + 2 demerits
  • 2nd Offense - Parent Notification + 4 demerits
  • 3rd Offense - Parent Notification + 1 day suspension + 6 demerits

LEVEL 3 - Acts of Disrespect and Dishonesty

  • Forgery
  • Theft
  • Cutting classes
  • Bribery
  • Dishonesty
  • Gambling
  • Lewd Act
  • Insubordination
  • Vandalism (if proven)

Penalties for Level 3 Offenses

  • 1st Offense - Parent Notification + 1 day suspension + 13 demerits
  • 2nd Offense - Parent Notification + 2 days suspension + 13 demerits
  • 3rd Offense - Parent Notification + 3 days suspension + warning for dropping from school roll + 13 demerits

LEVEL 4 - Acts of Unbecoming of an HIMC Student

  • Cheating
  • Any form of bullying
  • Hazing
  • Sexual harassment
  • Initiating fights or fighting
  • Extortion
  • Possession or bringing deadly weapons (bladed weapons, guns, etc.)
  • Possession, use, or sale of prohibited drugs
  • Possession, use, drinking, or sale of alcoholic beverages
  • Any act punishable by law

Penalties for Level 4 Offenses

  • 1st Offense - Parent Notification + 1 day suspension + 26 demerits
  • 2nd Offense - Parent Notification + 2 days suspension + Conduct probation + 26 demerits
  • 3rd Offense - Parent Notification + Removal from attending synchronous classes for the remainder of the quarter + warning for dropping from school roll + 26 demerits
